All About Minerva Applications and Examinations

  • Shariar Rahman
  • Jan 1, 2022

Real world problem-solving isn’t taught in a lecture hall. Four years, seven countries. The university for students with curiosity, ambition, and a drive to change the world. Join the Class of X. Rethink Higher Education. Bias-Free Admissions. No Application Fees.

Each of the five majors — Arts & Humanities, Business, Computational Sciences, Natural Sciences, and Social Sciences — has been designed to teach you the broad skills and concepts pertinent to that field, while enabling multiple paths to expertise via the concentrations within.

Application Timing:

Early Action: due November 01, Year.

Candidates notified by December 15, Year.

Regular Decision I: due January 17, Year.

Candidates notified by March 31, Year.

Regular Decision II: due March 15, Year.

Candidates notified by April 15, Year.

Extended Decision: due April 11, Year.

Candidates notified by April 26, Year.

Documents Need to Apply:

Valid Passport.

4 Years Academic Transcripts (grade 9 - 12 grade).

Extra-curriculum Activities/Achievement Details.

Counsilor Authorizations.

Any documents PDF file and refference Details(Ex. Name, Email, Phone Number, Link).

Briefly Explanation about Work and You.

Financial Documents (ex. Family valid Tax Certificate, Family Income Certificates, Salary Documents, Assets Details , House Pictures).

Examination Details:

You have to faces multiple questions sets. Like English Reading,English Writing, Creative, Mathematics(actually 9 grade maths),Reasoning Test, and Interview, but short times to cope up.

Examination Questions Sample:

Firstly you have to face English Quiz. Duration: 7 Minitues.



Then you have to face Creative Question. Duration: 8 Minitues.


Then you have to face Mathematics Quiz. Duration: 8 Minitues.


Then you have to face Reasoning Quiz. Duration: 20 Minitues.


Then you have to face English Writing. Duration: 20 Minitues.


Then you have to face Interview. You have to answer 6 Interview questions. Duration: 15-20 Minitues.
