Podcasts You Should Listen Before Applying US Colleges

  • Md. Sanaul Haque Shanto
  • March 31, 2022

Most of the students remain busy with their daily academic studies. Thereby it becomes hard for them to research universities they are going to applying. Even it becomes more complicated when it comes to where to look for information. That's somewhat that particular busy student needs guidance. If you are busy at the same point, did you ever thought of listening podcast? I am sure you hadn't. Yes, indeed, listening to podcasts is not common in our Bangladeshi student societies. I would say that give it a try. I am sure that you will love it. Here are some podcasts I mentioned below that will help you know more about USA college admissions processes and Scholarships for International students.

1. College Essay Guy:

Ethan Sawyer, known as the College Essay Guy and author of College Essay Essentials: A Step-by-Step Guide to Writing a Successful College Admissions Essay, has hosted 214 episodes of this podcast. Recent episodes have included diverse topics such as advice for homeschooled students and financial tips for international students. He also mentioned how to write unique essays, find out the right colleges and so many other things. I will also suggest you visit his website. You will surely find out something new for admissions.

Google Podcast

2. Inside the Yale Admission Office:

The podcast features interviews with former admissions officers, graduates from top universities, and admissions experts. each episode will be filled with application tips and first-hand experiences that will help you get into your dream school, no matter where you are in the admissions process.

Youtube Link:

Google Podcast.

Apple Podcast

3. A-Z of College Recruiting and Sports Scholarships for International Students:

As an international student, I think this is the best podcast to know more about how the admission process for international students works. The recent episodes of these podcasts cover the local scholarship and any short of unique opportunities for you. Recently they started to make podcasts around 5-8 minutes which will save your time too.
